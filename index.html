About Me

Building products that make the right thing the easy thing.

I'm a Product Manager with 6+ years of experience across B2B SaaS, HR Tech (HRMS), and Health Tech. At greytHR, I owned the HRIS/HRAS domain — a suite of 7+ products — during a period where the platform scaled 53% in customer base and 75% in revenue.

I hold a CSPO certification and recently completed AI for Product Discovery & Strategy and AI Prompt Engineering (Scrum Alliance, 2026), bringing an AI-augmented approach to discovery, prioritisation, and stakeholder communication.

I'm an active member of Toastmasters International and ProductTank — continuously sharpening both my product thinking and my ability to communicate it.

My PM Philosophy

I start with the problem, not the solution — and I stay curious until I find the real one underneath. I build products that make the right thing the easy thing, for everyone who touches them.

I am a product-led growth believer at heart — I think the best products earn their users through value, and keep them through the experience they deliver.
